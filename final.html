Welcome to Weld O Canada Inc.

Proudly Canadian, Weld O Canada Inc., a CWB-certified fabrication shop, operates out of a 22,000 sq. ft craned building fully equipped with fabrication equipment. We are certified for (CSA W47.1) in Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel (CSA W47.1), and Aluminum (CSA W47.2).

Weld O Canada is also certified for TSSA B31.1 (Power Piping), B31.3 (Process Piping), Fabrication, and Installation. We also provide mechanical contractor services. Our well-versed engineers, estimators, and trades provide seamless technical and customer support. Our safety program allows our staff to work efficiently with no time lost, instilling confidence in supporting your next project.

Weld O Canada is also certified in the quality manual ISO 9001:2015, and it has its own HSE Manual for safe operation.

Weld O Canada focuses on the fabrication of ASME pressure vessels, biopharmaceutical equipment, modular skids, vacuum chambers, storage tanks, and other custom-designed equipment for stainless steel applications. Industries served include biopharmaceutical, semiconductor, alternative energy, power generation, and other industrial applications.

Our clients consist of well-established and industry-leading firms that require exceptional precision and quality-engineered components delivered on time and at competitive prices.

With our ISO 9001 and TSSA certifications, along with CWB 47.1 & 47.2 and CSA-51 certifications, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and safety in all our projects.

Our Expertise

  • Welding and Fabrication: We specialize in providing welding and fabrication services for various industries, including power and process pipe welding, custom fabrication, and structural work.
  • Certification: Our TSSA certification for power and process pipe welding (B31.1 & B31.3) and CWB certification (47.1 & 47.2) demonstrate our expertise and compliance with industry standards.
  • CSA-51 Certification: As certified CSA-51 Boiler and Pressure Vessel welders, we possess the necessary skills and qualifications to handle critical projects in this field.

Why Choose Weld O Canada?

  1. Quality Assurance: Our ISO 9001 certification ensures that our processes are streamlined and focused on delivering exceptional quality in every project.
  2. Safety and Compliance: Our TSSA, CWB, and CSA-51 certifications guarantee that our work meets the highest safety and regulatory standards.
  3. Experience and Expertise: With years of experience and a team of skilled welders and fabricators, we have the knowledge and capabilities to handle complex projects.
  4. Commitment to Customer Satisfaction: We prioritize our clients’ needs and work closely with them to understand their requirements, ensuring that we deliver results that exceed their expectations.
  5. Timely Delivery: We understand the importance of meeting project deadlines and strive to complete projects on time and within budget.
